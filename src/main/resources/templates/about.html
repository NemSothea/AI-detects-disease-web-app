About Our Team

We're a group of developers passionate about plant health and agricultural technology.

Our plant disease detection application uses advanced machine learning algorithms to help farmers and gardeners identify diseases in their plants quickly and accurately.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, reliable plant health diagnosis tools that help improve crop yields and promote sustainable agriculture.

How It Works

  1. Upload a clear image of your plant's leaves
  2. Our AI model analyzes the image for disease symptoms
  3. Receive instant results with confidence scores
  4. Get recommendations for treatment if needed
